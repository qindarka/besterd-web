What We Do

Mechanical Systems & Services

Our fully licensed, TSSA-certified tradespeople handle the complete scope of commercial and industrial mechanical work — new construction, design-build, retrofits, and ongoing service. Every project is backed by our commitment to safety, schedule, and budget.

  • ❄

    Chiller & Cooling Systems

    Chiller installations, chilled water piping, condenser water systems, and cooling tower connections for commercial and institutional buildings.

  • 🔥

    Steam & Boiler Systems

    Steam boiler installations, steam and condensate piping, de-aerators, and boiler plant servicing for healthcare, manufacturing, and campus facilities.

  • 🏠

    Hydronic & Radiant Heating

    Hydronic heating distribution, in-floor and ceiling radiant systems for commercial and institutional new construction and retrofit projects.

  • 💧

    Gas & Medical Gas Piping

    Natural gas and medical gas piping designed and certified to code for hospitals, laboratories, and commercial facilities.

  • ⚙

    Process & Power Piping

    Power piping, stainless steel schedule pipe, fittings and valves, sanitary tubing, and complex process piping for industrial clients.

  • 💉

    Underground & Site Piping

    Riewil underground piping systems, water and sewage treatment plant work, and below-grade mechanical distribution.

  • ✅

    Backflow Prevention

    Certified installation and testing of backflow preventer devices for commercial, institutional, and industrial water systems.

  • 📈

    Design-Build & Fixed-Price

    Full mechanical design-build services with fixed-cost pricing. Infrastructure Ontario qualified. We manage the full scope so you don’t have to.

  • 🔨

    Service & Maintenance

    Service vehicles operating throughout London and area. Piping repairs, system commissioning, and scheduled preventive maintenance.

Our Story

60 Years of
Mechanical Excellence

Founded in London, Ontario in 1961 by William Besterd, we have grown from a small piping contractor into one of Southwestern Ontario’s most trusted names in commercial and industrial mechanical contracting. We’ve built our reputation the same way we build mechanical systems — with precision, professionalism, and an uncompromising commitment to quality.

Led today by President Ian Roff and General Manager Colin Cook, our team of fully licensed tradespeople brings deep expertise across the full range of commercial HVAC, piping, and mechanical systems. We are TSSA certified, affiliated with the United Association Local 593, and qualified to work with Infrastructure Ontario on public-sector projects.
