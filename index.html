❖ About Besterd Mechanical

Besterd Mechanical was founded in London, Ontario in 1961 by William Besterd. Bill’s strong work ethic and personal integrity built a company reputation for providing quality workmanship at a fair price. From humble beginnings with just six employees taking on gas station piping and school plumbing, the company has grown into one of the region’s trusted names in mechanical contracting.

Today, led by President Ian Roff and General Manager Colin Cook, Besterd Mechanical continues that same tradition. Our team of fully licensed tradespeople — TSSA certified and affiliated with the United Association Local 593 — brings decades of expertise to every project, from a single washroom to large-scale hospital and infrastructure builds.
