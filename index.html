❖ About the Shop

Besterd Mechanical opened its doors in 1961 when William Besterd hung out a shingle on a mechanical contracting business rooted in London, Ontario. Six decades later we are still here — still independent, still family-run, still turning wrenches on the buildings our neighbours work, learn and heal in.

Our crew of licensed plumbers, pipe fitters, sheet metal workers and HVAC technicians brings the same commitment to quality and safety to every job, whether that's a hospital expansion, a school retrofit or a process piping install at a manufacturing plant. We show up on time, we communicate, and we stand behind the work.
